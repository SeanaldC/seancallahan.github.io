About Me

I am a 4th year Economics Ph.D. student at Colorado State University. I describe myself as an international macroeconomist as I am interested in how the industrial composition of economies change over time and what policies can be used to steer this change.

CV Google Scholar GitHub

Research Interests

  • Macrodynamics of Income Distribution
    Studying the dynamic relationships between income distribution, economic growth, and structural change.
  • Ecological Transition Economics
    Analyzing economic policies and pathways for sustainable ecological transitions.
  • Industrial Policy
    Examining how policy interventions shape industrial composition and economic development.
  • Ecological Economics
    Investigating the intersection of economic systems and environmental sustainability.
  • Macroeconomic Theory
    Developing theoretical frameworks for understanding complex economic systems and networks.
